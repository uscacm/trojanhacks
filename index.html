Workshops

How to Hackathon

Thursday before the hackathon, we will be having a workshop to teach you the basics of not only how to survive a hackathon, but how to have a great and rewarding experience as well. Learn from the best and have successful future hackathons!

Google: How to Kick-start your career

Hear from Google representatives about how you can set yourself up for a successful career as a software engineer and learn insider information from a seasoned professional that is working for an industry-leading company themselves!

How to Web Development

Learn the fundamental skills of HTML/CSS and Javascript, so you can launch into making power web applications and websites for a versatile and boundless hack that anyone can access and share from their laptops.

How to Unity

Advance your skills in Unity Game Engine and learn the fundamentals of game development so you can begin to create and imagine your own games.

How to Chatbot

Make a customized chatbot that will cater to your hack's needs and audience, through this workshop that will teach you the basics of creating this unique feature.

How to Android

Have you always wanted to make a mobile application, but didn't know where to begin? Learn the basics of Android development to make one of the most versatile hacks!

How to iOS

Delve into a new world of programming through this workshop, which will teach you the basics of Swift and XCode to get you started on hacking in iOS.
